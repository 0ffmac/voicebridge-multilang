# VoiceBridge — User Guide

> **Translate. Connect. Communicate.**

VoiceBridge is a voice translation app that works in two ways:

| Mode | Icon | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Live Translation** | 🎙️ | Translate speech in real time — perfect for face-to-face conversations |
| **Voice Messaging** | 💬 | Send translated voice messages to friends anywhere in the world |

> **ℹ️ Note:** No account is needed for the Live Translator. Login is only required for Voice Messaging.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Opening the App

Open your browser and go to:

```
https://voicebridge-multi.pages.dev
```

| Platform | Browser to use |
|----------|---------------|
| 📱 iPhone | **Safari** |
| 🤖 Android | **Chrome** |
| 💻 Desktop | Chrome or Edge |

The Live Translator opens immediately — **no login required.**

---

### 🎤 Allowing Microphone Access

The first time you tap the microphone, your browser will ask permission. Tap **Allow**.

![VoiceBridge Login Screen](screen_login.png)
*The login screen — tap "← Back to Translator" to use the app without an account*

> ⚠️ If you accidentally denied mic access, go to browser **Settings → Site Permissions → Microphone** and allow VoiceBridge.

---

## 🌐 Live Translator

The Live Translator is the main screen. Use it when you are physically with someone who speaks a different language.

![VoiceBridge Translator](screen_translator.png)
*The main translator screen with language selectors and mic button*

---

### 🗣 Selecting Languages

At the top of the screen you see two dropdown menus:

- **Left dropdown** → the language you will **speak in**
- **Right dropdown** → the language you want to **hear the translation in**
- **⇄ button** → instantly **swap** both languages

---

### 📱 iPhone (Safari) — How to Translate

1. Tap the **🎙️ microphone** button — it shows ⏹ and says *"Listening"*
2. Speak clearly in your chosen language
3. After **3.5 seconds of silence** it stops automatically and translates
4. Button changes to **🔊** and says *"Tap to hear translation"*
5. Tap the button again to **hear the translation spoken aloud**
6. Tap again to start a **new translation**

> 💡 You can also tap ⏹ manually at any time to stop recording early.

---

### 🤖 Android (Chrome) — How to Translate

1. Tap the **🎙️ microphone** button — it shows ⏹ and says *"Listening"*
2. Speak clearly — Android **auto-detects** when you stop speaking
3. Translation appears on screen and is **spoken aloud immediately**
4. Tap the microphone again to translate something new

---

### 🎛 Buttons Reference

| Button | Name | What it does |
|--------|------|-------------|
| ▶ | **Replay** | Plays the translation aloud again |
| ⎘ | **Copy** | Copies the translated text to clipboard |
| ✕ | **Clear** | Clears the original text and translation |
| ⇄ | **Swap** | Swaps source and target languages |
| 👥 | **Contacts** | Opens your contacts list *(login required)* |
| 🔑 | **Login** | Opens the login screen |
| 👤 | **Profile** | Edit your profile and language preference |
| ⏏ | **Logout** | Log out of your account |

## 🔑 Creating an Account

An account is required to send and receive voice messages. VoiceBridge uses **magic links** — no password needed.

### ✉️ How to Sign Up

1. Tap the **👥 Contacts** or **🔑 Login** button
2. Enter your **email address** and tap **Send Magic Link**
3. Check your **email** for a message from VoiceBridge
4. Tap the login link — ⚠️ **it expires in 15 minutes**
5. You are taken to the **profile setup screen**

> 💡 Check your spam folder if the email doesn't arrive within a minute.

---

### 👤 Setting Up Your Profile

1. Enter your **display name** (real name or nickname)
2. Enter a **username** — letters, numbers and underscores only, minimum 3 characters
3. Select your **preferred language** from the dropdown
4. Tap **Get Started**

> ⚠️ Your preferred language is important — it tells VoiceBridge what language to translate **incoming messages into** for you. Make sure it's set correctly.

## 💬 Voice Messaging

Voice messaging lets you send translated messages to friends anywhere in the world. You speak, VoiceBridge translates, your friend receives the message in their language.

![VoiceBridge Contacts](screen_contacts.png)
*The contacts screen — search for friends and manage requests*

---

### 👥 Adding Friends

1. Tap the **👥 Contacts** button
2. Type a **username** in the search bar *(minimum 2 characters)*
3. Find the person and tap **+ Add**
4. They receive a **friend request**

---

### ✅ Accepting Friend Requests

1. Tap the **👥 Contacts** button
2. Pending requests appear at the top under **FRIEND REQUESTS**
3. Tap **✓** to accept or **✕** to decline

---

### 🎙️ Sending a Voice Message

1. Tap a **friend's name** in your contacts to open the chat
2. Tap the **🎙️ microphone** and speak in your language
3. VoiceBridge **automatically translates** into your friend's language
4. Your friend receives the message and can tap **▶** to hear it

> ℹ️ The translation language is always your friend's preferred language from their profile. If they update it, future messages translate automatically — no action needed from you.

---

### 📩 Receiving Voice Messages

1. Open the **chat** with your friend
2. New messages appear **automatically every 8 seconds** — no refresh needed
3. You see the **translated text** in your language
4. Tap **▶** on any message to **hear it spoken aloud**

> 💡 If you scroll up and new messages arrive, a blue **"↓ N new messages"** badge appears at the bottom. Tap it to jump to the latest.

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### 🗣 Speaking Clearly

- Speak at a **normal, natural pace** — not too fast or slow
- Avoid **loud background noise** when possible
- Hold the phone **close to your mouth** in noisy environments
- Speak **one sentence at a time** for the most accurate translations

---

### 📱 iPhone Tips

- Always use **Safari** — Chrome on iPhone uses Safari's engine underneath
- The flow requires **two taps**: one to record, one to hear the translation
- If the microphone stops responding, **close the tab** and reopen the app
- Make sure your **phone volume is turned up** to hear the translation

---

### 🤖 Android Tips

- Use **Chrome** for the best experience
- Translation happens **automatically** after you stop speaking — no second tap needed
- If Chrome asks for microphone permission, always tap **Allow**
- Works on both **WiFi and mobile data**

---

### 💬 Voice Messaging Tips

- Make sure your friend has set their **preferred language correctly** in their profile
- Keep messages **short and clear** for the most accurate translation
- Tap **▶** on any message to hear it again at any time
- The app checks for new messages every **8 seconds** automatically

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| 🔴 *"Mic error: not-allowed"* | Go to browser **Settings → Site Permissions → Microphone** and allow VoiceBridge |
| 🔴 *"Translation failed: Failed to fetch"* | Check your **internet connection** and try again |
| 🔴 *"Speech recognition not supported"* | Use **Safari on iPhone** or **Chrome on Android**. Firefox is not supported |
| 🟡 App stuck on *"Loading..."* | Hard refresh: **Cmd+Shift+R** on Mac, or pull down to refresh on mobile |
| 🟡 Magic link not arriving | Check your **spam folder**. Links expire in 15 minutes — request a new one |
| 🟡 Friend not appearing in search | Search requires **at least 2 characters**. Check the exact username spelling |
| 🟡 Messages not updating | Chat checks every **8 seconds**. Wait a moment or leave and re-enter the chat |
| 🟡 Translation in wrong language | Ask your friend to check their **profile language setting** |

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Data

- 🎙️ **Voice stays on your device** — speech recognition runs in your browser and is never sent to VoiceBridge servers
- 📝 **Only transcribed text** is sent for translation
- 🛡️ **Messages are stored securely** in a Cloudflare database with encryption at rest
- ✉️ **Your email** is used only for login and is never shared or sold
- 🚫 **No ads** — VoiceBridge does not show advertisements or track you
